Cementery personnel were amazed when the exhumed the mummified bodies.

Soil in Guanajuato is rich in minerals enabling the body to go through a natural mummification process. One of the first mummified bodies was found in the early 1800's when the cementery administration ordered the exhumation of bodies whose families couldn't afford to pay the tax to keep their loved ones buried.
